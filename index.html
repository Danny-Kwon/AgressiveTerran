How to play starcraft with terran?

There are three kind of races in the starcraft, and these are Terran, Zerg and Protoss. I want to explain you about how to play terran, because it's my favorite! There are so many kinds of build, and you need to choose whether you want to play aggresive or not. But this document is gonna show the way to play aggresive. And before we start, let me introduce this race first.
According to the storyline, Terrans are mankind who are abandoned from their society. Units are including disabled, criminals, cyborgs and mutations. They are banished from Earth, and navigatied through universe with there spaceship. After the long trip, they finally settled on Koprulu Sector. Each of individual units are pretty weak, but they never give up before their last man die. In professional league of Korea, this race produced many talented gamers.   • Agressive Builds

    It means, as literally, the game strategies that playing as we don't live for tomorrow. I recommend it if you are interested in teasing enemy to concentrate on their defense systems. Both of you would be very poor, but you can achieve victory in short time.

    BBS

    BBS is an abbreviation of "Barracks-Barracks-Supply Depot", you can use it to beat all kinds of races. Send your 6th scv and 8th scv to the center field of the map and build barracks there. You don't have to produce more than nine scvs. After building two barracks, send those scvs to search where's enemy base. When five marines are called-up, invade the enemy and set the rally of your barracks to enemy's front yard. Then, build bunker and win the battle!

    8 Barracks

    8barracks is a build that you building the barracks when your population number is 8. You may build the barracks on your front yard. After 2 or 3marines are produced, you can go on a rush. This build is usually used against zerg, and your purpose is canceling hatchery on enemy's front yard.

    Two factories

    This strategy is used against terran or protoss. You need to build two factories before vitalizing your double command center. It's very important not to show your strategy to the enemy, and act like you already vitalized the multi base. You can go on a rush after 3 seize tanks are manufactured. Research for the mine as soon as possible, and research for the seize mode while producing vultures. It will be effective if you have some marines.

    Fake double

    This strategy is used against protoss. It requires you to deceive your enemy. They might send their part of army to patrol and earn some information from you. You need to act as getting the multi command center on your front yard. But, after manufacturing the factory, you need to research on mines and go on a rush when one seize tank and six marines are engaged. Your purpose of this rush is make your enemy to abandon their front yard at least. If you fail to achieve this goal, your game will be hard to play.

    Two barracks one academy

    You can choose this tactic when you finish the enemy zerg in 10 minutes. It's similar to what I mentioned right before. Build two barracks, but you must not show your second barracks. You need to build academy ASAP and research the steam-up. After all these procedures finished, go on a rush and seize the victory.
